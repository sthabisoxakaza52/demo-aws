## 1. AI Recommendation Engine (Events & Networking)

## 2. LinkedIn Scraping & Opportunity Integration

* **Targeted Keyword Scraping:** The system **must** periodically scrape LinkedIn for job openings, requests for proposals (RFPs), and business opportunities that match the user’s portfolio and industry tags.
* **Lead Deduping & Scoring:** The system **must** filter out duplicate listings and score opportunities based on how closely they align with the entrepreneur’s stated business goals.
* **External Profile Syncing:** The system **must** allow users to pull relevant public data from their own LinkedIn profiles to fast-track their app portfolio setup.

* **Grant & Investor Aggregator:** The system **must** maintain a searchable database of active funding opportunities, venture capital firms, and government grants.
* **AI-Assisted Pitch/Proposal Generator:** The system **must** generate structured business proposals, grant applications, and investor pitch decks based on the user's business metrics and traction data.
* **Traction Milestone Tracker:** The system **must** allow users to input and visualize key performance indicators (KPIs) like user growth, revenue, or milestones to showcase to potential funders.

* **Digital B2B Showroom:** The system **must** allow entrepreneurs to host a public-facing digital portfolio or product catalog to showcase their offerings to prospective clients and employers.
* **Traction Proof Badging:** The system **must** verify and display specific business achievements (e.g., "Seed Stage Completed", "1k+ Users reached") on the user’s portfolio.

## 5. User Management & Onboarding

* **Role-Based Profiles:** The system **must** support distinct onboarding flows for **Entrepreneurs** (looking for growth/funding) and **Partners/Investors/Employers** (looking to fund or hire).
